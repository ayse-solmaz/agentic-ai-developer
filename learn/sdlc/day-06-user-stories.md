## User stories

### Story 1: Upload a song and see its hit score
- User uploads a song to the system.
- System analyzes the song's metrics and produces a hit score.
- User sees the song's hit score.

### Story 2: Find the highest-scoring song among multiple songs
- User uploads multiple songs to the system.
- System scores each song and shows which one has the highest hit score.

## Epic split

**Epic:** Hit Bowling — Song Hit Scoring System

- Story A: Upload a song and see its hit score
- Story B: Compare multiple songs to find the highest-scoring one

## Priority order

1. Story A — Upload a song and see its hit score
2. Story B — Compare multiple songs to find the highest-scoring one

**Why this order:** Story B depends on Story A — to compare multiple songs and find the highest-scoring one, the system first needs to be able to score a single song accurately. Building Story A first creates the foundation Story B is built on.
